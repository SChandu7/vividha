Help & Support
📦 What is your shipping policy?
Free shipping on all orders above ₹999. Standard delivery takes 5-7 business days. Express delivery available at checkout.
🔄 What is your return policy?
Easy 7-day returns on all products. Item must be unused and in original packaging. Refund processed within 3-5 business days.
🔍 How do I track my order?
Enter your order number in the Track Order section below. You'll receive SMS updates at every stage of your delivery.
💳 What payment methods do you accept?
We accept UPI, Credit/Debit cards, Net Banking, and Wallets via Razorpay. All payments are 100% secure and encrypted.

Each product page has a detailed size guide. For sarees, one size fits all. For stitched items, refer to the measurements chart on the product page.

About विVIDHA
EST. 2024 · INDIA'S FINEST WOMEN'S FASHION

विVIDHA was born from a simple belief — every woman deserves to wear art. We curate India's most exquisite ethnic and fusion wear, handpicked from master weavers and artisan workshops across the country.

From the silk looms of Kanjivaram to the embroidery ateliers of Kashmir, every piece in our collection tells a story of centuries-old craftsmanship meeting contemporary design.
